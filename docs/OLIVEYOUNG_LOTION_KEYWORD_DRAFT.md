## Notes

- This draft uses Korean commercial phrasing on purpose to test locale behavior.
- `무료배송` was treated as a merchandising signal, but deeper promo claims were kept conservative.
- `화잘먹`, `속광` are high-value Korean ad terms because they reflect real shopping intent language, not only literal product taxonomy.
- This case is useful because it shows the generator may need to support market-language output even when direct page acquisition is inconsistent.
